Healthy Blueberry Zuccini Muffins!

These muffins are the perfect snack to appease one's sweet tooth . They are easy to bake and filled with vegetables and wholesome ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups All purpose gluten free flour or chickpea flour
  • 1/4 Baking Soda
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1 1/2 cups Shredded Zuccini
  • 1 tsp Maple Syrup
  • 2 tsp Vanilla and Almond Extract
  • 1/2 cup Greek Yogurt
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups Blueberries

Instructions

  • Prep time: 20 minutes
  • Cook time: 25 minutes
  • Serving size: 12 muffins
  1. Preheat your oven to 350ºF
  2. In a small bowl, stir together the dry ingredients; flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon.
  3. In a large bowl, mix in the wet ingredients; beat together the eggs, greek yogurt, and vanilla, until smooth.
  4. Slowly stir in the flour mixture until just incorporated. Fold in the zucchini and blueberries.
  5. Scoop 1/4 cup batter each and distribute into muffin pan.
  6. Bake at 350ºF for 25-30 minutes, until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Cool for about 5 to 10 minutes and enjoy.
